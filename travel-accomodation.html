Travel & Accomodation

Accomodation

There is a limited number of rooms available in the venue hotel for a discounted rate (approximately 100 Euro/day) at the time of early registration. There are also many other accommodation options in the vicinity of the conference venue, you can make a booking through the registration system. You can find more information on the available hotels in the following document.

Hotel reservations are possible until March 5th, 2015. Payments for the hotels should be made by March 5th, 2015 (at this date, the unpaid reservations will be cancelled). After this date reservations are possible on request only (subject to room availability). With such requests please contact karolina.turlej@globalwings.pl.
